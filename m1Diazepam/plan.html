Goals

The main goal of this website is to promote the store to generate sales by updating customers on the latest events and providing an online order service.

Success Evaluation

The success of the website can be evaluated by comparing the sales and the attendance of the events before and after the website is launched.

Target Audience

The product is available for all ages but the main target audience is for younger people and parents who wants to buy for their kids. The website’s design will be bright, attractive and user-friendly to cater to this target audience.

Content

  • Home Page
    • This page is the first thing that the customer will see. It contains a navigation bar to link to other parts of the website and some brief description of the store.
  • About Us
    • Contains story about the founder of Diazepam why and how he created the company along with a description about what the company does for its customers.
  • Product Page
    • Shows the customers the products that are available with pictures displaying each product.
      • Ice-cream (Single/Double scoop)
      • Waffle ice-cream
      • Ice-cream cake
      • Fried ice-cream
      • Ice-cream cookies
      • Ice-cream sundae
      • Ice-cream float
  • Contact us
    • Contains the contact information for the company via email or phone that a customer can use to contact the store.
  • Events
    • Shows a list of upcoming events that are going to happen including brief details of each event sorted by their date.
  • Cafe
    • Provides information about the upcoming café with images to entice their customers and details on the opening date and promotions.
  • Services
    • Provides information on delivery details and free delivery, party planners will get free flow of ice-cream and details about exchanges and returns.
  • Order Online
    • This page provides an online order form for customers.
  • Feedback
    • This page allows customers to rate the store's services and provide feedback for improvements.
  • FAQ
    • This page provides answers regarding customers' frequently asked questions.
